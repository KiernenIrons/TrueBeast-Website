IMAGES FOLDER
=============
Drop your images here. The website references these files:

  hero-banner.jpg      - Large banner/background image for the hero section
  about-photo.jpg      - Your personal photo shown in the About section
  featured-game.jpg    - Featured game/highlight image in the Content section

Recommended formats: .jpg, .png, .webp
Recommended sizes:
  hero-banner   → 1920x1080px (16:9)
  about-photo   → 600x600px (square)
  featured-game → 640x360px (16:9)
